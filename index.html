Welcome to Shuu Italia

The answer to all of your Shuu needs!

Established in 1990, Shuu Italia has strived to produce high quality footwear at affordable prices over the past three decades. Shuu Italia has something for everyone, be it footwear for Ladies, Gents or Kids, we have it covered.

Looking for something special? Browse our Accessories page for those extra luxuries! We offer premium leather belts, hats & handbags all at amazing prices. Designer Italian shoes are extensive in collections and give you a felling that they are made exclusively for you.

Italian designers are the best in the world when it comes to shoes. They ensure that the design is great and the comfort is not lost at all. These shoes are made of the softest leather that is available and manufactured with 100% perfection to every detail of the shoe – particularly the comfort.

Most often, when shoes are manufactured to be more fashionable they don’t last long and also they are not very comfortable to wear. On the contrary, Italian shoes are not just fashionable, they are the most comfortable shoes one could imagine irrespective of the fact that they also involve handmade decorative work with the finest leathers and in the best of the colors.
